Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 19, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you use Spendly, we collect only the information needed to run the Service. This includes your account details (name, email address, and a hashed password) and the expense data you choose to enter — amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions.

We may also collect basic technical information such as your browser type and the pages you visit within Spendly, used solely to keep the Service secure and reliable.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used to provide the Service to you — displaying your transactions, generating category breakdowns, and powering features such as monthly summaries and date filters.

We do not sell your data, and we do not use it to build advertising profiles. We will only contact you for account-related matters or, with your consent, for product updates.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on servers we operate. Passwords are stored only as salted hashes — never in plain text. We retain your expense data for as long as your account is active so that you can review your history.

You may request deletion of your account and associated data at any time, and we will remove it within a reasonable timeframe, except where we are required by law to retain certain records.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly may rely on a small number of third-party services to operate — for example, hosting providers and, if enabled, email delivery services. These providers only process the minimum data required to perform their function and are bound by their own privacy and security standards.

We do not share your personal financial data with advertisers, data brokers, or any third party for marketing purposes.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or how your data is handled, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.app. We will respond to reasonable requests regarding access, correction, or deletion of your data.
